Busan

Gwangalli Beach has a white sand beach curved in a gentle half-moon shape with high-quality sand from Mt. Geumnyeon, and a nearby tourist and entertainment complex is also developed. The coastal road along the beach is lined with restaurants, cafes, and raw fish restaurants that create a unique atmosphere. It is a popular tourist attraction in summer.
