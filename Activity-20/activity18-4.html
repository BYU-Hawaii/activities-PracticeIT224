How to Play

Here are some guides to help you get started with our games:

    Game 1: Start by clicking on the play button. Use the arrow keys to navigate.
    Game 2: Match the shapes by dragging and dropping them into the correct slots.
    Game 3: Solve the puzzles by arranging the pieces in the right order.
