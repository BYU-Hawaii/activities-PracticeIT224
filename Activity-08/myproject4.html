Seoul

N Seoul Tower (Namsan Tower) is a representative landmark in Seoul where you can enjoy a panoramic view of Seoul. It serves as an observatory and radio transmission tower, and because it is located on Namsan Mountain, geographically located in the center of Seoul, you can enjoy a 360-degree panoramic view of the beautiful scenery of Seoul. N Seoul Tower is one of the popular attractions that both domestic and foreign travelers must visit, and is also well-known as a romantic date spot among couples.
